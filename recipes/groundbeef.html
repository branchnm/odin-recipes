Ground Beef

------Ingredients------

  • Kirkland Organic 85/15 Ground Beef
  • Salt/Pepper

------Steps------

  1. Take the beef out of the packaging, and chop up in a cast iron pan.
  2. Turn heat to high and let brown, stiring often.
  3. Wait until brown, then add salt and pepper, and then let simmer.
  4. Once done, use a slotted spoon to scoop just the beef into another bowl and enjoy!
