Fried Rice

This is my favorite type of meal. This glorious plate of chicken fried rice, with egg whites and veggies is an amazing combination of healthy and delicious foods. Here's how to make it!

------Ingredients------
  • Egg Whites
  • 56g White Onion
  • 10g Minced Garlic
  • 15g Ginger Past
  • 224g Chicken Breas
  • 240g Cooked Jasmine Rice
  • 30g Light Soy Sauce
------Steps To Cook------
  1. Spray PAM
  2. Cook the eggs whites
  3. Once cooked, put into separate bowl and combine, ginger, onions, and garlic.
  4. Wait to brown the onions, then add chicken.
  5. Once Chicken it cooked throuh add the rice.
  6. Finally, add the soy sauce to the rice, finish cooking, and enjoy!
