Mashed Potatoes

------Ingredients------

  • Russet Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Whole Milk
  • Salt and Pepper

------Steps------

  1. Peel, wash, and cube potatose
  2. Then put into a big pot with water near potatoe line
  3. Bring to boil, then let simmer until potatoes soften
  4. Once cooked, drain with colander and add 1tbps of butter per potatoe, salt, pepper, and .1cup of milk per potatoe.
  5. Mash and mix until done, tasting as you go.
  6. Let sit and enjoy!
